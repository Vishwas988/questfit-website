Transforming Fitness into an Adventure.

This document outlines the requirements for **QuestFit**, a platform designed to make physical activity delightful, rewarding, and social. Explore the core concepts and features that will drive user engagement and build lasting healthy habits.

Target Audience

QuestFit is primarily for fitness beginners and casual exercisers who are motivated by goals and social rewards, with a secondary audience of data-savvy individuals looking for new motivational tools.

Core Website Features

From seamless onboarding to effortless activity tracking, every feature is designed with simplicity and user motivation in mind. The goal is to remove friction and make fitness tracking a natural part of the user's day.

🚀 Swift Onboarding

A setup process under 90 seconds. We ask for minimal data and integrate with health platforms for immediate value.

👤 The "Adventurer" Profile

A fun, customizable avatar profile that showcases levels, streaks, and badges, turning progress into a visual identity.

🏃‍♂️ Automatic Tracking

Automatic pedometer, distance, and workout detection for common activities like walking, running, and cycling.

✍️ Simple Manual Entry

Easily log over 50 activities from Yoga to Weightlifting with simple duration and intensity inputs.

📊 Visual Dashboard

A clean dashboard that displays daily, weekly, and monthly progress with engaging charts, not just boring numbers.

🧠 Adaptive Coaching

The platform provides smart goal suggestions and encouraging push notifications to keep users motivated without being annoying.

Focused on Your Goals: Weight Loss

QuestFit provides specialized tools and visuals to support your weight loss journey, making progress clear and motivating.

🍎 Calorie & Nutrition Tracking

📈 Visual Progress Reports

Don't just see numbers. Visualize your weight loss journey with beautiful charts that show your progress over time, celebrating every milestone.

Technical Foundation & KPIs

A successful platform requires a robust technical foundation and clear metrics for success. We are focused on performance, security, and measurable user engagement.

Non-Functional Requirements

  • ⚡️
    Performance: Platform loads under 2s with smooth animations.
  • 🎯
    Usability (UX): Minimalist UI. Key actions require 3 taps or less. Self-explanatory onboarding.
  • 🌐
    Accessibility: Adherence to WCAG 2.1 AA standards, including screen reader support.
  • 🔒
    Security: All user data encrypted in transit and at rest with a transparent privacy policy.
  • 📈
    Scalability: Backend architected to support 1 million+ active users without performance degradation.
